How It Works

Three strategies,
one yield

01
Funding Rates

Delta Neutral

Long spot paired with short perpetuals on HyperCore to earn funding payments. Market-neutral, no price beta.

02
Lending

Supply Spot

Lending via Felix, Hyperswap, and HyperLend to generate additional yield from lending markets.

03
Liquidity

Idle Buffer

~5% TVL maintained as reserve for smooth redemptions. Withdrawals process within approximately one day.

Security

Battle-tested
and audited

Multiple independent audits, multisig governance, proven vault framework.

Triple Audited

Independently audited by Verichains, Shieldify, and Zenith. All reports publicly available.

Multisig Governance

Safe multisig (3-of-5) with timelock on all sensitive parameter changes.

BoringVault Framework

Modular vault architecture with Merkle tree authorization and role-based access control.
